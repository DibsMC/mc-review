Review Budz

You can request permanent deletion of your Review Budz account at any time.

Delete directly in the app (fastest)

  • Open Review Budz.
  • Go to User tab.
  • Scroll to Delete account.
  • Confirm deletion.

Request deletion by email

If you cannot access the app, email us from your account email address: dibsmccallum@sky.com

Include your sign-in email and the subject line: Account Deletion Request.

What is deleted

  • Sign-in account (Firebase Authentication).
  • Your profile record and personal account data in the app database.

What may be retained

  • Reviews may be anonymised for community integrity and safety moderation logs.
  • Operational logs required for security, fraud prevention, and legal compliance may be retained for a limited time.
