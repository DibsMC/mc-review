Privacy Policy

Review Budz

Effective date: 13 February 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Review Budz ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Review Budz mobile app (the "App").

What we collect

  • Email address (for sign-in).
  • User-generated content such as reviews, ratings, notes, and any reports you submit.
  • Identifiers such as your Firebase user ID (UID) to associate your account with your content.

What we do not collect

  • We do not collect precise location.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos library, camera, or microphone unless you explicitly use an OS share/save flow.
  • We do not use analytics tracking SDKs for advertising or cross-app tracking.

How we use your information

  • To create and maintain your account and sign you in.
  • To display your reviews and ratings in the App.
  • To prevent abuse and maintain community safety (e.g., handling reports).
  • To export your data when you request "Download My Data".

Where your data is stored

Review Budz uses Google Firebase services:

  • Firebase Authentication (email/password sign-in)
  • Cloud Firestore (storing products, reviews, and user profile data)

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data with service providers only to operate the App (e.g., Firebase).

Retention

We keep your account and content until you delete your account, or until we remove content that violates our Terms.

Your choices

  • Delete account: You can delete your account within the App. This removes your profile and associated data.
  • Download your data: You can export your data within the App.

Children

The App is not intended for children.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: dibsmccallum@sky.com

If you make changes to this policy, update the effective date above.
