# Review Budz Catalogue Source Coverage Estimate

Last reviewed: 26 March 2026

## Purpose

This note is a planning estimate for how much of the live Review Budz flower
catalogue can be retained and enriched using only approved public sources:

- official manufacturer pages
- official grower pages
- public pharmacy product pages

Excluded from this estimate:

- patient-only portals
- clinic-only portals or clinic formularies
- third-party directories whose terms prohibit scraping or database reuse

## Live baseline

- Live products exported from Firestore: **455**
- Products still missing terpene data: **270**

## Confidence estimate

- Best estimate: **around 330 flowers**
- 75% confidence range: **310 to 350 flowers**
- Conservative planning floor: **about 280 flowers**

## Coverage score legend

- **Strong**: public official product pages or datasheets are clearly visible
- **Medium**: official range/brand coverage exists, but SKU detail is partial
- **Weak**: public official information exists at company level, but little or no clear SKU detail

## Planning readout

The major takeaway is that Review Budz should not collapse from 455 flowers to a
tiny catalogue if we move to a defensible public-source model. The strongest
publicly supported brands already account for a meaningful chunk of the live
catalogue, and a further portion should remain supportable through public
pharmacy product pages and public producer datasheets.

The number is unlikely to stay at the full 455 without relying on questionable
sources, but the current evidence suggests a retained catalogue in the low-to-mid
300s is realistic.
