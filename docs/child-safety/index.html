Review Budz

Child Safety Standards

Review Budz has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE), child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sexualisation of minors, or any content or behaviour that endangers children.

In-app reporting available
Admin moderation enabled
Last updated: 23 March 2026

What is prohibited

  • Any CSAE or CSAM content in reviews, messages, profiles, or uploaded media.
  • Any attempt to sexualise, exploit, groom, or endanger a minor.
  • Any content that encourages, normalises, or facilitates abuse of children.
  • Any attempts to use Review Budz to contact, target, or manipulate minors.

How users can report concerns

  • Users can report reviews directly inside the app.
  • Flagged content is surfaced to admin moderation tools for review.
  • We may remove content, restrict accounts, or ban members where needed to protect users.

Moderation and enforcement

We review reports and moderation signals as quickly as reasonably possible. Where content or behaviour appears to breach child safety standards, Review Budz may remove the content, suspend or permanently ban the account involved, preserve relevant moderation evidence, and escalate the matter to appropriate law enforcement or child safety organisations where required by law or platform policy.

Age-appropriate use

Review Budz is intended for adults discussing their own lawful medical cannabis experiences. It is not intended for children. Accounts or content that suggest misuse of the app by or against minors may be restricted or removed.
