What we use for product facts

  • Official manufacturer or grower product pages.
  • Public pharmacy product pages where factual fields are openly available.
  • Publicly accessible producer datasheets or specification pages where permitted.

What we do not copy

  • Third-party editorial descriptions or marketing prose.
  • Images owned by external directories, clinics, or producers without permission.
  • Site structure, proprietary taxonomies, or searchable mirror databases from other services.

How provenance works

Where possible, Review Budz keeps a source name, source URL, source type, and a last-checked date for catalogue updates. This makes it easier to review where a factual field came from and when it was last checked.

How we keep this defensible

Review Budz avoids copying editorial descriptions, images, or searchable database structures from third-party services. Factual fields are reviewed against permitted public sources and tracked with provenance where possible.

Product names and factual fields are compiled from publicly accessible manufacturer, grower, pharmacy, and other permitted sources. All reviews, ratings, and effect data in Review Budz are user-generated or original to Review Budz.

If a source owner asks us to correct or remove factual data, we review that request and update the catalogue where appropriate.
